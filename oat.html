Oat Milk Recipe

Home

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup - old fashined oats
  2. 3 cups - water
  3. 1/2 tsp - salt

optional ingredients:

  • vanilla extract
  • sugar or alternative sweetner

Supplies:

  1. blender
  2. cheese cloth
  3. jar or lided container
  4. bowl
  5. colinder

Oatmilk

Step 1: add water and oats to blender, blend for 15ish seconds
Step 2: line a colinder/bowl with cheese cloth, strain the mixture
Step 3: transfer oatmilk into a jar or lided container, save the blended oats for baking
Step 4: add salt to the oatmilk, optional add preffered sweetner
